Who is Tracy Chou?

Tracy Chou is an entrapeneur, software engineer and advocate for diversity in tech proffisions. Also, CEO of Block Party, which is for building tools for online saftey and anti-harassement. She was an engineer and tech lead at Pinterst and before that, at Quora from 2011-2016, leading many important roles. Tracy's main spreading point is diversity and inclusion for everyone in tech.

Contributions To Computer Science:

Tracy Chou's contributions in computer science was more about diversity and inclusion and pressuring larger companies to disclose their diversity data. She is a great activist for diveristy throughout all tech proffesions and roles. Tracy has been raising profiles of the low representation of women in technology companies.
